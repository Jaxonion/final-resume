Producer App

Producer App is an application that helps music producers and songwriters alike. I've been working on trying to write lyrics and thought it would be helpful to have an extra resource for brainstorming as well as listening to songs as examples. One page helps songwriters brainstorm lyrics and the other helps producers hear popular songs of the selected genre in order to get a feel for how to compose.

Made with HTML, CSS, React, PostgreSQL, Javascript, Express, Node, Datamuse and Spotify APIs.
